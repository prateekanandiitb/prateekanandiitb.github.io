Welcome! I am an **Assistant Professor** in the **Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Bombay**.

My research focuses on **microhydrodynamics**, **inertial migration**, and the **dynamics of anisotropic particles** in viscous and inertial flows.
I combine theoretical and computational methods to study particle–fluid interactions and transport phenomena.

You can find details of my **publications**, **research projects**, and **group activities** using the links above.
