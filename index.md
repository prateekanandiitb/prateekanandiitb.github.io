## Research snapshot
We work on three overlapping themes:

- **Inertial migration & transport** — particle trajectories, equilibrium positions, and segregation in channel flows.  
- **Anisotropic particle dynamics** — orientation, tumbling, and lift of rods, spheroids, and fibers.  
- **Computational methods & open tools** — efficient solvers and reproducible codes for particle-resolved simulations.
